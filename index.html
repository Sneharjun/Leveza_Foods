ABOUT US

Leveza Foods – Freshness Made Simple

Great food isn’t just about taste—it’s about trust. At Leveza Foods, we blend quality, convenience, and reliability to redefine frozen food solutions. What began in 2008 as a small poultry distribution business has grown into South India’s leading provider of poultry and food solutions, catering to homes, restaurants, and retailers.
With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we’ve earned the trust of industry leaders and established a strong foothold in the HoReCa segment. As we continue to expand, our mission remains the same: to make great food effortless while being a trusted name in every kitchen and dining table.

WHY CHOOSE US???

Premium Quality Assurance

Leveza Foods conducts consistent quality control activities across the entire product journey, from source procurement through packaging operations, to protect the freshness and security of all products.

Ready-to-Cook Convenience

Time efficiency drives our ready-to-cook products and ready-to-eat products by combining easy meal preparation with delicious eating experiences.

Wide Scale Distribution

With 30+ reefer vehicles and warehouses across Kannur, Calicut, Malappuram, Palakkad, and Kochi, we guarantee fresh and timely deliveries across South India.

OUR SERVICES

Import & Export Excellence

Leveza Foods sources the finest ingredients from around the world to deliver premium-quality products to our customers. From authentic ethnic spices to superior cuts of meat and globally inspired flavors, we bring the best of international cuisine to your table—ensuring quality, freshness, and taste in every bite.

Wholesale Solutions

Leveza Foods offers a dedicated wholesale service, providing restaurants, hotels, and retail outlets with high-quality frozen products in bulk at cost-effective rates. Our wholesale solutions ensure businesses have access to premium ingredients, maintaining freshness, consistency, and value for large-scale culinary needs.

Efficient Distribution Network

Leveza Foods ensures the seamless delivery of fresh frozen goods across South India through our well-established customer delivery system. With strategically located warehouses and a dedicated fleet of reefer vehicles, we guarantee efficient, timely, and temperature-controlled distribution to meet the highest quality standards.

Retail Excellence

Leveza Foods brings everyday convenience to your kitchen with our premium retail range, featuring juicy chicken cuts and ready-to-cook delights. Available at leading distribution centers, our products ensure authentic flavors and top-quality freshness, making it easier than ever to enjoy great-tasting meals at home.
